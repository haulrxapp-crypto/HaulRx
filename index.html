Our Services

HaulRx is a marketplace platform that connects customers who need junk removal services with professional, local hauling companies. We provide the technology to facilitate these connections efficiently.

Cancellation, Refund, and Dispute Policy

HaulRx serves as a connection platform. We do not handle payments, refunds, disputes, or cancellations for the actual hauling services rendered. These arrangements are made directly between the customer and the hauler.

  • For Customers: All payments for junk removal are handled directly with the hauling company. Any issues regarding service quality, pricing, cancellations, or refunds must be resolved with the hauler you hired.
  • For Haulers: Hauling companies pay a subscription fee to access the HaulRx platform and its leads. A refund for this subscription fee can be requested within 30 days of the initial subscription start date by contacting our customer service.
